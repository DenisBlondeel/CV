Company: Forward

Abstract
As grabbing the attention of customers is getting harder and harder, the retail sector is looking to personalize the advertisements the people see. ln order to achieve this goal, the retail sector is trying to implement artificial intelligence in their so called "smart billboards". This project successfully implemented a way for a camera hidden in the frame of the billboard to label, to classify a person based on three different parameters: Age, gender and facial expression.
